Claire Bennett

Claire is Scottish. She's 31 years old. Claire speaks English and German and she's a doctor in a hospital in Edinburgh. She works at night during the week and at the weekend. She drinks a lot of coffee. How many hours does she work? 60 hours a week. Claire's job is difficult but she loves it because she helps people. Claire isn't married. She lives with her sister, Megan, and they go to the gym together on Tuesdays.

Marc Dubois

Marc is 26 years old and he's from France. He speaks four languages and his American accent is very good. What does Marc do? He's an actor and he lives in New York, but he also travels a lot for his work. He doesn't do a lot of exercise because he loves his car and in his free time he drives around the city. What car does he have? It's small, black and very fast!

Sofia Vargas and Elena Cruz

Sofia likes her job but Elena doesn't want to be a hairdresser. What does she want to do? She loves animals so in the evenings Elena studies to be a vet at college.
